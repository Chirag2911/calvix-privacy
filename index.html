Privacy Policy — Calvix

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Calvix (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a mobile application published by rechkiller (package: com.killerrech.CalvixAi). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use Calvix.

1. Information we collect

Account information. If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, display name, and a unique user ID through Google Firebase Authentication.

Health and nutrition information. You may enter or generate data including meals, calorie and macro estimates, water intake, body weight, goals, and saved favorite meals. When signed in, this is stored in Google Firebase Firestore. In guest mode, data is stored on your device.

Photos. If you use photo scan, a resized image of your food is sent to Google’s Generative AI (Gemini) to estimate nutrition. Signed-in users may optionally store a compressed meal thumbnail in Firebase Storage.

Voice. If you use voice logging, your device’s speech recognition converts speech to text. That text is sent to Google Gemini for analysis. We do not store audio recordings on our servers.

Subscription. Purchase and renewal status is processed by Google Play and RevenueCat. We store whether you have an active Pro subscription and its expiration date.

Advertising. Free users may see banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device and advertising-related data as described in Google’s policies.

Device data. We store preferences, offline sync queues, and guest-mode data locally on your device.

2. How we use information

  • Provide calorie tracking and AI meal analysis
  • Sync your data across devices when signed in
  • Manage Pro subscriptions
  • Send local meal reminders (if enabled)
  • Show advertising to free users

Medical disclaimer: Calvix provides nutrition estimates only. It is not medical advice. Consult a qualified professional for health decisions.

3. Third-party services

We do not sell your personal information. We share data with service providers only as needed to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, optional image storage — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini — AI food analysis — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — advertising (free users) — Google Ads Policy
  • Google Play Billing — subscriptions — Google Play Terms
  • RevenueCat — subscription status — RevenueCat Privacy

4. Guest mode vs signed-in

In guest mode, your data stays on your device until you sign in. When you sign in, guest data may be migrated to your cloud account. You can sign out at any time.

5. Your choices

  • Use guest mode without signing in
  • Deny camera, microphone, or notification permissions in device settings (some features will not work)
  • Cancel a Pro subscription in your Google Play account settings
  • Contact us to request access to or deletion of your account data

6. Data retention

We retain account data while your account is active. Uninstalling the app removes local device data. Cloud data remains until you request deletion.

7. Children

Calvix is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) and Firebase access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate, including the United States.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.

Account and data deletion

To request deletion of your Calvix account and associated data, email killerrech@gmail.com from the same Google account email you used to sign in to Calvix.

Include in your email:

  • Subject: Calvix Account Deletion Request
  • Your Google account email used in Calvix
  • Confirmation that you want your account and cloud data deleted

What we delete: Your Firebase account data, including profile settings, meal history, favorites, daily stats, and stored meal thumbnails.

What may remain: Google Play subscription and purchase records are managed by Google and may be retained per Google’s policies. AdMob may retain advertising-related data per Google’s policies.

Timeline: We process verified requests within 30 days.

Guest mode: Uninstalling the app removes local guest data from your device. No cloud deletion request is needed.
